## 🎯 Target Audience

### Primary Segments

#### 1. Fitness Beginners (35% of users)
- **Demographics:** 25-45 years old, any gender
- **Goals:** Lose weight, get healthier, build habits
- **Pain Points:** Don't know where to start, intimidated by gyms
- **Needs:** Guidance, encouragement, simplicity

#### 2. Casual Exercisers (30% of users)
- **Demographics:** 25-55 years old, any gender
- **Goals:** Maintain fitness, track progress
- **Pain Points:** Inconsistency, lack of motivation
- **Needs:** Reminders, variety, social accountability

#### 3. Serious Lifters (20% of users)
- **Demographics:** 18-40 years old, predominantly male
- **Goals:** Build muscle, track PRs, progressive overload
- **Pain Points:** Need detailed tracking, hate clutter
- **Needs:** Efficient logging, history, analytics

#### 4. Runners/Cardio Focus (15% of users)
- **Demographics:** 25-50 years old, any gender
- **Goals:** Improve endurance, race training
- **Pain Points:** GPS accuracy, integration with existing tools
- **Needs:** GPS tracking, training plans, Strava sync
