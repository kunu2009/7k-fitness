## 🎯 Quick Reference

### Our Mission
> Create a fitness app that combines the best of all competitors while avoiding their mistakes, making fitness accessible to everyone.

### Our Differentiators
1. **Customer-First Support** - 24-hour response guarantee
2. **Honest Pricing** - No tricks, easy cancellation
3. **All-in-One** - Workout + Nutrition + Social + AI
4. **Universal Compatibility** - Works with all devices
5. **Community-Driven** - Features users actually want

### Key Competitors to Watch
| Competitor | Watch For |
|------------|-----------|
| MyFitnessPal | Food database improvements |
| Strava | Support improvements |
| Strong | Nutrition features |
| Nike Training Club | Monetization changes |
| Fitbit/Google | AI capabilities |

### Timeline Summary
```
Q4 2025: Foundation (Exercise DB, Goals, Health sync)
Q1 2026: Engagement (Programs, Notifications)
Q2 2026: Nutrition (Food tracking, Scanner)
Q3 2026: Social (Friends, Challenges)
Q4 2026: AI & Premium Launch
2027+:   Scale & International
```
